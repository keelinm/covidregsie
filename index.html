This website provides visual guides to COVID-19 testing and isolation rules in the Republic of Ireland.

The information contained on this website is an interpretation of the official guidelines listed on the HSE website and partly based on flowcharts made by HPSC Ireland. While I have made every effort to ensure the information here is accurate, you should consider the HSE website as the official source of information.

By publishing these visual guides I am NOT endorsing or approving the official HSE guidelines. Please do NOT contact me to point out inconsistencies which exist in the original HSE recommendations.
